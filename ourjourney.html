Milestones

Our Story, Year by Year

From our founding to becoming a multi-award-winning global platform, every step has been guided by purpose, principle, and innovation.

2006
Establishment

Founded to support Malaysia's vision of becoming a global Islamic finance hub, pioneering Commodity Murabahah using palm-based assets.

2007
First World Trade

Successfully executed the world's first Commodity Murabahah trade using agro-based commodities.

2010
Web Transition

Transitioned from manual operations to a fully web-based Commodity Murabahah trading platform.

2011
Middle East Entry

Expanded internationally by welcoming our first Middle Eastern financial institution.

Future
Continuous Innovation

Continuing to innovate ARIIMS through enhanced security, scalability and next-generation Shariah-compliant digital solutions.

Today & Beyond
